About this website

This website is a work-in-progress attempt to implement, adopt and, adhere to the indieweb guiding principles. I've been actively creating content for media companies that sell advertising for a long enough time. These silos have created likewise silos of thought, polarizing reality dramas, all unfolding like a grimdark novel with the backdrop of an ever-escalating climate disaster. If the modern social media landscape is grimdark then the indieweb is the hopepunk. The IndieWeb principles:

  • ✊ Own your data
  • 🔍 Use & publish visible data
  • 💪 Make what you need
  • 😋 Use what you make!
  • 📓 Document your stuff
  • 💞 Open source your stuff!
  • 📐 UX and design is most important
  • 🌐 Modularity. Build platform agnostic platforms
  • 🗿 Longevity. Build for the long web
  • ✨ Plurality. Embrace a diversity of approaches & implementations
  • 🎉 Have fun

That is what I'm here for.
